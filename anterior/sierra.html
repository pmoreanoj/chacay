La Sierra, The Mighty Andes

The Ecuadorian Andes region, commonly known as "La Sierra", includes ten provinces, each with its own distinct flavor and culture diversity. The Andean Highlands cover only 20% of the surface of Ecuador, but are home to nearly 50% of the Ecuador population, most of them in the UNESCO cultural heritage sites of Quito and Cuenca, both striking colonial cities.

We have an assortment of day trips that we like to include into the beginning of your stay in Ecuador. Through these quick trips you will be able to ease into Ecuador and gain a feel for the country before we delve further into the country's wonders!
