Encuentros for high school students

Encuentros 2012: In the Footsteps of Giants, offers extraordinary opportunities for high school students to experience first hand the wonders of the most bio diverse country, per square meter, in the world: Ecuador, a country of superlatives and a diamond in the rough! Visits include all four of the country's regions: la Amazonia, la Sierra, la Costa, y Galapagos!

Activities include adventure (hiking, biking, canyoning, rafting, bungee jumping, surfing), Spanish language instruction and practice during home stays, up to 80 hours of community service, and now the option of a week on Galapagos, led by qualified guides and youth leaders, in the context of cultural exchange, transform our participants into curious, confident, and committed youth leaders. Guaranteed: the metamorphosis of a lifetime! Sign up today for an early bird discount, valid through the end of February 2012.
