The CHACAY team

Dr. David Lansdale, the founder and President of CHACAY, is Director of the Center for Entrepreneurship and a professor at Ecuador´s leading liberal arts university, the Universidad San Francisco de Quito.  Born and raised in Thessaloniki, Greece, David is a graduate of Mount Hermon School in Massachusetts, Oberlin College in Ohio, and Stanford University´s School of Education, with a doctorate in Educational Administration. His passion is promoting Ecuador as world class destination to tourists interested in more than just an extraordinary experience: his vision is to enable the country and its people to realize their extraordinary potential where you, as a CHACAY client, can become an agent of change!


Marco Diaz, CHACAY’S General Manager in Ecuador, is also Credit Manager at ETA Fashion, the country’s leading clothing retail store.  He and his family live in Quito, but his family roots are in Cuenca while his wife, Jenny, comes from Ibarra, two of Ecuador’s most picturesque colonial cities.  Marco and his family host multiple visitors from around the world, and on several occasions, have adopted North American students who have come to Ecuador as high school students, and during their the gap year.  He and Jenny have also visited the United States and Canada, and extend a cordial invitation to visitors from around the world!



Pablo Romero, our Director of Operations, is a native of Quito, and has dedicated the last ten years to tourist activities both within and outside Ecuador. He loves his country, its biodiversity, and his experiences have produced a passion and desire to share Ecuador and its wonderful nature with others from around the world. Pablo enjoys practicing adventure sports, especially Mountain Biking. Pablo looks forward to meeting you, and to coordinating an unforgettable experience for you that might include the picturesque Pacific Coast, the Avenue of the Volcanoes in the mighty Andes region, the extraordinary Amazon headwaters, and the Enchanted Islands, in the footsteps of Darwin!



Ingrid Krantz is CHACAY’S Unites States Business Manager.  She has visited Ecuador, has brought down visitors on several occasions, and has been smitten by the kindness and generosity of the Ecuadorian people, as well as by the physical beauty of the country.  She has worked for both Apple and Silicon Valley Graphics, and is a seasoned traveler (has visited five continents) and a professional photographer.
