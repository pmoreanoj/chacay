Chacay University Expeditions

Chacay extends the invitation to design your onsite program in Ecuador, and will provide the access to university faculty and other renowned content experts who will help you create an extraordinary case study that will include side trips to the country's most renowned destinations. Areas include business administration and social entrepreneurship, social work, public health, nursing and medicine, biology, ecology, volcanology, anthropology, and Latin American history and culture Each area will include complementary site visits.


Sample University Program Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Quito, airport welcome, and transfer to your boutique hotel
Day 2: Orientation, visit to Universidad San Francisco, introduction to area experts ranging from anthropology, biology and ecology to volcanology and zoology; Spanish language instruction; afternoon and evening in old colonial Quito
Day 3: Morning content area classes and Spanish instruction, afternoon excursion to the Middle of the World and the Pacific Cloud Forest
Day 4: Morning content area classes and Spanish language, afternoon spent ziplining, hiking, tubing and biking, with a visit to the Butterfly Farm (Mariposario)
Day 5: Morning special classes and Spanish language. Return to Quito for afternoon teleférico trip to 2800 meters and hike up to Rucu Pichincha
Day 6: Morning special classes and Spanish language. Afternoon trip to Baños, with visit to Quilotoa Crater
Day 7: Morning special classes and Spanish language. Biking, canyoning, bungee jumping, rafting, zipline
Day 8: Morning special classes and Spanish language. Afternoon return to Quito via Cotopaxi, climb to refuge
Day 9: Morning special classes and Spanish language. Trip to Papallacta Hot Springs in the paramo of the mighty Antisana volcano (spot a condor?); return to Quito for Chiva bus ride through the city, farewell party
Day 10: Drop off at the airport, return trip home
