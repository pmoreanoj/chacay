LA COSTA

The provinces of Esmeraldas, Manabí, Guayas, El Oro and Los Rios, make up the Ecuadorian coastline with more than 2,000 kilometers of beautiful beaches, ports and fishing villages. The entire Coastal Area provides an vast array of exhilarating and relaxing experiences ranging from paragliding to snorkeling to whale watching. The weather here is an average of 25ºC (76º F) to 30ºC (90º F). From December to May is the rainy season (Winter), when the weather is usually warmer. The dry season is between April and November, cooler and less humid. New and diverse experiences include visiting local communities, sailing, whale watching, sport fishing or biking along the coast. Chacay guarantees unforgettable experiences, based on warm hospitality and an introduction to one of the Ecuador's most distinctive qualities: its cuisine!
