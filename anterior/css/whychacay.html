Why Chacay?

Chacay in Quichua, the language of the Inca, means building bridges. We provide and guarantee personalized, quality and affordable experiences at top rated boutique hotels and on luxury yachts in Ecuador and Peru. Top-notch guides, fluent in English, will introduce you to the multiple facets of the world's most exquisite diamond in the rough!

With offices in Quito, Ecuador's capital, and Los Gatos, California, Chacay extends a cordial invitation to North Americans, Europeans, and Asians to discover Ecuador, a multifaceted, multicultural and bio diverse wonderland, and Machu Picchu, one of the seven wonders of the modern world. You also have the option, when you return home, to help polish this diamond in the rough through scholarships, investments in microenterprise, or as an enthusiastic promoter, a Chasqui (the Inca messenger), of the country.
