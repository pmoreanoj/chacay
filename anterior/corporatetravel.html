Corporate Responsible Experiences in Ecuador

Chacay's corporate responsibility division organizes extraordinary experiences for multinational, regional, and national companies interested in socially responsible corporate retreats. We will work with you to identify interests, priorities and expectations, and will then create a tailored package that best fits your needs. Whether you want to visit the Amazon, Sierra, Coast, Galapagos or all of the above, we can make it happen!

Our extensive network of alliances with local and national foundations ensures that our clients receive the very best quality in service, coupled with a highly personalized introduction to Ecuador. As we say at Chacay - Ecuador is the world's best kept secret: an exquisite diamond in the rough where your corporation can truly make a difference!
